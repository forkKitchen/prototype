Ingredients

  1. 4 racks pork spare ribs
  2. 2 ½ cup Frank's Red Hot Sauce
  3. ¼ cup ground black pepper
  4. ½ cup paprika
  5. ½ cup chili powder
  6. 2 tbsp cayenne pepper
  7. 1 tsp MSG
  8. 2 tbsp salt
  9. 2 tbsp curry powder
  10. 2 tbsp garlic powder
  11. ½ gallon apple cider vinegar
  12. 2 cups generic BBQ sauce

Steps

  1. Prepare smoker with hickory or apple wood, and maintain temperature at 230 F. Keep water tray filled with apple cider vinegar.
  2. Slather the ribs in 2 cups of Frank's, reserve remaining.
  3. Combine dry ingredients and coat ribs
  4. Place ribs in smoker for three hours, maintaining steady smoke
  5. Remove ribs from smoker, cut into half slabs. Baste each half slap with BBQ sauce and reserved Frank's.
  6. Place each half slab on a large sheet of heavy foil. Create basin with edges, and add half cup of apple cider vinegar. Wrap and seal ribs in foil, then replace in smoker.
  7. Maintain temperature at 230 F with or without additional smoke.
  8. Prepare gas or charcoal grill with medium heat, approximately 350 F
  9. Remove ribs from smoker and unwrap. Grill for 30 seconds on each side and remove to platter. Serve immediately with a bunch more Frank's.
